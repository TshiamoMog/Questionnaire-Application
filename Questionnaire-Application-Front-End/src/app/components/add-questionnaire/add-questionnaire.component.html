Form Title

Section

A section will group questions to a division or categorization within a larger form questionnaire. It's a way of organizing related questions under a common theme or topic.

Multiple Choice

The respondent will be presented with a statement or question, followed by several options or choices, for the respondent to then select the correct answer from.

Multiple Choice (Check-Box)

The respondent will be presented with a statement or question, followed by several options or choices, for the respondent to then select the multiple correct answers from.

Ranking

The respondents will be asked to order or prioritize a set of options based on the question specifications.

Rating

The respondents will be asked to provide a rating based on a predefined scale like 1 - 5, 1 - 10 or 0 - 100%.

Date

The respondents will be required to provide a specific date, represented by a calendar, to capture information such as their birthday.

Likert

The respondents will be presented with a statement and asks them to indicate their agreement or disagreement using a scale.

Open Ended

The respondents will be asked to provide a detailed and personalized response, with provided options as answers to the question.

Upload

The respondents will be required to submit a file or document as part of their response to the question.
